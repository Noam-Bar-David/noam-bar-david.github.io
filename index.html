Career & Projects

Research positions, lab work, and collaborative digital‑humanities projects.

2024–present
Assistant Lecturer
Bar‑Ilan University
Teaching courses on digital documentation, GIS, and aerial mapping for the humanities, alongside guest lectures on digital and computational humanities.
2022–present
Researcher, E‑lijah Lab for Digital Humanities
University of Haifa
Development of NLP and machine‑learning tools for Ancient Greek, and large‑scale digital processing of ancient texts within ISF‑funded projects.
2022–present
Junior member, RIAB Minerva Center
Bar‑Ilan University & University of Leipzig
Participation in interdisciplinary research on urbanization, political hierarchy, and writing practices in the Iron Age Levant and later periods.
2018–present
Technology & Documentation Coordinator, Tell es‑Safi/Gath Excavations
Bar‑Ilan University
3D documentation of archaeological excavations using LiDAR, multispectral imaging, photogrammetry, UAV‑based aerial photography, and GIS database management.
2020
Area Supervisor, Tel Abu‑Shusha (Gaba)
Excavation field school
Supervision of excavation area, architectural interpretation, and spatial documentation.
